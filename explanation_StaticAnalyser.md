# Static Analyser — Simple Explanation for Everyone

## What is this project?

**Static Analyser** is a tool that checks software projects and tells teams where problems may exist **before those problems become costly**.

Think of it like a **health check-up for code**:
- It looks at a project carefully
- It finds risky or weak areas
- It explains what should be improved

---

## Motto (Core Purpose)

The motto of this project is:

> **“Find issues early, explain them clearly, and help teams build safer, better software.”**

In simple words, this project is made to:
- reduce hidden mistakes
- improve software quality
- save developer time
- lower business risk

---

## Vision (Long-Term Goal)

The vision is to build a smart analysis platform that can become a **trusted assistant for software teams**.

That means:
- not just finding problems, but also helping teams understand *why* they matter
- giving useful suggestions that are easy to act on
- supporting teams in making steady quality improvements over time

---

## Why does this matter for non-technical people?

Even if you are not a developer, this project helps in business outcomes:

- **Fewer production bugs** → better customer experience  
- **Stronger security awareness** → lower chance of incidents  
- **Better code quality** → easier and faster feature delivery  
- **Clear reporting** → easier decision-making for managers and stakeholders

---

## How it works (non-technical view)

At a high level, the system does four simple things:

1. **Reads the project**
2. **Checks for different kinds of risks** (security, performance, architecture, test gaps)
3. **Combines findings into a single report**
4. **Shows understandable results** in a dashboard

So instead of manually checking thousands of lines, teams get a guided summary of what needs attention first.

---

## In one sentence

**Static Analyser’s purpose is to make software quality and risk visible, understandable, and actionable for both technical and non-technical teams.**
